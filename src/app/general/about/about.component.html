About Mysupermarket:

What is mySupermarket?

mySupermarket is more than a price comparison site.
We are completely independent from the supermarkets,
helping shoppers save 30% on average on each grocery shop.
Our free to use site aims to help shoppers save money on their grocery shop.
We were established in 2006 to provide shoppers with a wealth of information to make pricing,
promotion and product information as transparent as possible for shoppers.
As a consumer champion we are proud to work for the customer to ensure they get the best value for their household, on each and every shop.
